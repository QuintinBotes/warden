01 — Identity

The portcullis mark

A gate of falling bars — the medieval warden's last line of defense. It is the logo, and the same shape becomes the verdict in the hero. One idea, doing two jobs.

Warden
16
24
32
Construction
  • Seven bars, spiked feet. Reads as a gate at 16px and as a chart at 320px — the bars double as pass/fail columns in the UI.
  • Arched keystone. The frame never moves; only the bars rise (open) or fall (block).
  • Gold at rest, status when live. The mark is beacon-gold in chrome; in a verdict it takes the gate's status color.

03 — Type

Console voice, prose warmth
